Kimberly Knight
Principal Designer  ·  Twig & Trove Interiors

Kimberly Knight founded Twig & Trove Interiors on a simple belief: the most beautiful spaces are never purely of one era, one source, or one idea. They are built slowly and deliberately, with deep respect for craftsmanship and an eye always open to the world.

Her work spans high-end residential projects across Dallas and the nation, from intimate family homes to landmark club spaces. She layers natural materials with antique and contemporary pieces in service of spaces that feel personal, timeless, and enduring.

Kimberly is also recognized as an editorial stylist for national shelter publications, with features in Traditional Home Magazine, Luxe Magazine, and other Meredith Publications. Disciplined, image-forward, and deeply attentive to the story a room tells, it is a sensibility that informs every space she designs.
